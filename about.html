Our History

CivilWorks Civil Engineering Contractors was founded in 2007 by an experienced partnership of civil engineers. The management team has a combined experience of more than 40 yearsin the industry covering a broad rangeof sectors including highways, municipal infrastructure, buildings and irrigation systems.

Our commitment to excellence has been the driving force behind establishing CivilWorks as the contractor of choice throughout the sectors they serve.The combined efforts of our professional management team and highly skilled workforce continuously strive to generate the best results for our clients.

Clients

United Nations Development Programme

Renovations of existing microbiology laboratories to meet WHO standards and guide lines as described in “good practices for pharmaceutical quality control laboratories (1) and ISO/IEC 1702(5)”.

United Nations High Commission For Refugees

Construction of a guest lodge, two staff houses and one admin block. Complete with electrical and mechanical specialist services. Repairing of the water reticulation system at the camp including installation of water booster pump and storage reservoir tanks..

United Nations Operations

Construction of outpatients waiting areas and renovation of existing OI clinics at Mberengwa hospital, Mvuma hospital, Gokwe hospital and Zvemabande hospital. The works included demolitions and reconstruction of walls to create new space in existing structures ,painting ,electrical works, water and sewer reticulation and plumbing repairs.

St Francis of Assisi Mission

The project involved the construction of a single block with four , 2 bedroomed apartments for the teaching staff.

SESANE / CRIDF

Contruction of a 7km pumping main: sinking of four sand boreholes in Thuli River and erection of solar panels to power the projects.

PUMA Energy

Rebranding of eight service stations. Works included construction of cut-off drains, oil interceptors, MID bases, renovations and painting of forecourt concrete slabs. Service stations included Amtec Gweru, AMC Kadoma and Arman Kwekwe

United Nations Food Program Agency

The project involved the refurbishment & reconstruction of mothers waiting homes at the 3 health centres.

CCI Church

Construction of a four-storey framed structure which consists of office blocks and a five thousand seater auditorium. The structure is sitting on 0.8m thick raft foundations. The roof is light gauge steel trusses with IBR sheeting.

ZEGU University

Construction of a four in one sports court. The works included excavation, back filling, compaction, court pavement formation and erection of fence and accessories.

Zimbabwe Power Company (ZPC)

The project involved the refurbishment of an existing kitchen to a 100 seater staff canteen & associated plumbing works.
