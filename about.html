Our History

CivilWorks Civil Engineering Contractors was founded in 2007 by an experienced partnership of civil engineers. The management team has a combined experience of more than 40 yearsin the industry covering a broad rangeof sectors including highways, municipal infrastructure, buildings and irrigation systems.


Our commitment to excellence has been the driving force behind establishing CivilWorks as the contractor of choice throughout the sectors they serve.The combined efforts of our professional management team and highly skilled workforce continuously strive to generate the best results for our clients.
